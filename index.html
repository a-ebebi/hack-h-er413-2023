HER+Market

HER+Market is a community-driven tool that helps you find sustainable food options in your area. We prioritize partnering with women-owned farms and farmers' markets, particularly those owned by women of color, to promote sustainability and equity in the food industry. Our platform provides information on the environmental impact of different foods and suggests eco-friendly recipes to inspire your cooking. Join our community and start eating sustainably today!

Resources for Sustainability and Supporting People of Color

  • Buy from local farmers' markets
  • Reduce food waste by planning meals and using leftovers
  • Choose organic, non-GMO foods
  • Grow your own food
  • Support women-owned and BIPOC-owned businesses
  • Donate to organizations supporting sustainable farming and food justice
  • Volunteer with community gardening and food access programs
